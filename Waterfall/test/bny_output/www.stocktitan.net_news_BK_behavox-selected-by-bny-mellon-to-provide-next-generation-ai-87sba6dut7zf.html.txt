Head of Global Compliance Assurance, at BNY Mellon noted. “Our experience is off to a good start with Behavox’s responsiveness, flexibility, and commitment to high standards of quality giving us confidence in them as a long-term partner.”\n\n\nBNY Mellon joins a growing list of prestigious institutions in the financial sector that have selected Behavox Quantum to elevate their compliance communications monitoring programs.\n\n\nNabeel Ebrahim, Chief Revenue Officer at Behavox said, “Behavox is honored to work with an organization like BNY Mellon, which values technological innovation and compliance with regulatory commitments. We are confident in delivering and expanding our solution at BNY Mellon.”\n\n\nBNY Mellon will be implementing Behavox Quantum, which recently completed its one-year anniversary. BNY Mellon had $45.7 trillion in assets under custody and/or administration and $1.8 trillion in assets under management as of September 30, 2023. BNY Mellon has been named among Fortune's World's Most Admired Companies and Fast Company's Best Workplaces for Innovators.
