best insurers in the United States,\" Ondo CEO Craig Foster.\n\"Nationwide have been the perfect anchor partner for us in the United States and by taking the lead in the geographic expansion of our solution, make it much easier for other US carriers to also introduce our groundbreaking product to their customers across the United States.\n\"The fact that the production capacity to handle demand
