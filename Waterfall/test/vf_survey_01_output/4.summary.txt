"Participants expressed overall satisfaction with the course structure, pacing, and content. Particularly, sessions on AI Ethics, Design Thinking, and \"Achieving Personal High Performance\" were highly beneficial.\n\nThe component on Large Language Models and Generative AI was especially appreciated. However, Python coursework could be more supportive, suggesting drop-in coding sessions and solution walkthroughs.\n\nFeedback indicated the need for improved user feedback during the \"Gateway Projects\" build phase. Participants valued the Udacity course for its adaptability, especially helpful for those less proficient in Python coding."
