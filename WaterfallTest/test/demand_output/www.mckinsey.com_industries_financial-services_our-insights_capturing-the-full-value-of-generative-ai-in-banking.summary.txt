"Generative AI in banking has the potential to bring annual value of up to $340 billion, but scaling and adopting the technology is a challenge. It can enhance customer engagement, content generation, and coding. Data and analytics are crucial, as well as talent-related challenges. Organizations with AI expertise have an advantage. Successful scale-up requires capabilities in various areas such as talent, data, technology, and governance. Education, transparency, and addressing concerns about job losses are important. Integration between technical talent and business leaders is crucial, as well as considering technology options and regulatory implications. Challenges in leveraging unstructured data, ensuring data quality, and addressing risks related to generative AI must be addressed. Financial institutions can benefit from Gen AI, but adoption and change management are crucial for successful implementation and full adoption in the market."
