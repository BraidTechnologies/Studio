"Infosys has announced a collaboration with NVIDIA to develop high-performance generative AI-powered telco solutions. The collaboration will leverage Infosys' AI-first services and solutions, called Infosys Topaz, and NVIDIA's NIM inference microservices to enhance customer experiences and streamline network operations for telcos. The solutions include TOSCA Network Service Design, an AI-powered networking operating center, and a contact center transformation platform."
