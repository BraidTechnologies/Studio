"Generative AI has the potential to widen or reduce the racial economic gap in the US, with Black Americans potentially capturing only a fraction of the wealth it creates. Automation may lead to job loss and worsen disparities. However, gen AI can also address healthcare access, banking services, and racial gaps. To ensure fairness, equity needs to be incorporated into AI development, training for at-risk workers, regulating AI use, promoting unbiased data and diversity, and involving stakeholders in gen AI design."
