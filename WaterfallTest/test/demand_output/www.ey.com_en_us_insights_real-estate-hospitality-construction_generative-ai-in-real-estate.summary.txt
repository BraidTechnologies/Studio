"Generative AI is transforming the commercial real estate industry by improving efficiency, identifying risks, and creating new business models. However, it also comes with challenges such as impact on the workforce, transparency, accuracy, bias, cybersecurity, and fraud. Implementing GenAI requires a specific approach, including selecting use cases, transforming processes, and ensuring responsible and ethical practices. Companies should also create guidelines and a governance framework, upskill employees, and utilize foundational elements of the technology stack."
