"EY India discusses the future of consulting in the age of Generative AI. They explore the impact of AI on the consulting industry and how it can enhance efficiency and improve client outcomes. Consulting firms have a significant role in driving the adoption of Generative AI technology, offering powerful solutions and capabilities. Generative AI is seen as an opportunity to improve decision-making, problem-solving, collaboration, innovation, and data-driven insights. It is not seen as a threat to jobs but rather a tool for creating new roles and upskilling. EY's digital transformation teams are incorporating Generative AI to help businesses evolve and seize opportunities. Consulting must engage with organizations to drive innovation and forge alliances."
