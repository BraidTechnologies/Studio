"Microsoft and NVIDIA are partnering to accelerate generative AI for enterprises. The integrations include adopting NVIDIA Grace Blackwell Superchip, integrating NVIDIA DGX Cloud with Microsoft Fabric, and introducing NVIDIA generative AI microservices for Microsoft Azure AI. These collaborations aim to bring the power of AI to customers and improve productivity. The partnership also focuses on advanced AI capabilities in the Azure cloud platform, collaboration in healthcare and life sciences, industrial digitalization, and using NVIDIA GPUs and Triton Inference Server in Microsoft Copilot. The goal is to enhance AI deployment and empower users with improved capabilities. NVIDIA's microservices powered by AI software help accelerate the development of AI applications. NVIDIA is known for accelerated computing, while Microsoft is known for intelligent cloud and edge solutions."
