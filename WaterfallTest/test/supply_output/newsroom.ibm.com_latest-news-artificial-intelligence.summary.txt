"IBM is collaborating with the World Wide Fund for Nature - Germany to develop an AI solution for monitoring endangered species. They are starting with African forest elephants. IBM is also partnering with ASMPT to advance bonding methods for chiplet packages for AI. They have made their Mistral Large model available on watsonx.ai and are working with AWS to promote responsible AI adoption. IBM has reaffirmed its commitment to the Rome Call for AI Ethics, a collaboration with religious leaders to shape ethical AI development. The commitment was made at the AI Ethics for Peace event hosted by the Vatican."
