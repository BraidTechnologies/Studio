Start learning          Back to RESOURCES section                Frequently asked questions about Azure OpenAI Service        Expand all     Collapse all              01/       What is the price for using Azure OpenAI Service?               Azure OpenAI Service offers pricing based on both Pay-As-You-Go and Provisioned Throughput Units (PTUs). Pay-As-You-Go allows you to pay for the resources you consume, making it flexible for variable workloads. PTUs, on the other hand, offer a predictable pricing model where you reserve and deploy a specific amount of model processing capacity. This model is ideal for workloads with consistent or predictable usage patterns, providing stability and cost control.
