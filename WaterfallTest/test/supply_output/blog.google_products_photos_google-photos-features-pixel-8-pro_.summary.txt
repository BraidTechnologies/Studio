"Google Photos has added four new AI-powered editing features to the Pixel 8 and Pixel 8 Pro, including Best Take for group photos, Magic Editor for easy photo editing, Audio Magic Eraser for reducing distracting sounds in videos, and Zoom Enhance for focusing on specific parts of a photo. These features will be available from October 12. Additionally, Google has announced the release of the Pixel Buds Pro 2, but no further information is provided about the product."
