"ServiceNow, NVIDIA, and Accenture are partnering on the AI Lighthouse program to advance the use of generative AI in businesses. By leveraging ServiceNow's platform, NVIDIA's AI computing and software, and Accenture's consulting services, they aim to drive automation, self-service, and content generation. The collaboration focuses on IT service management, customer service management, and employee experience, as well as developing chatbots and search capabilities. The partnership aims to improve workflows and agent productivity in the ServiceNow platform."
