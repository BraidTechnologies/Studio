"Adobe and NVIDIA are collaborating to develop advanced generative AI models for creative workflows. This partnership will enhance Adobe's Creative Cloud products and introduce the new NVIDIA Picasso cloud service, aiming to improve creative options and speed up content production. Adobe is also working on generative AI services for video and 3D asset creation and has founded the Content Authenticity Initiative. NVIDIA is developing custom generative AI models called Llama 3.1 and has announced \"NVIDIA AI Computing by HPE\" with Hewlett Packard Enterprise to accelerate the generative AI revolution."
