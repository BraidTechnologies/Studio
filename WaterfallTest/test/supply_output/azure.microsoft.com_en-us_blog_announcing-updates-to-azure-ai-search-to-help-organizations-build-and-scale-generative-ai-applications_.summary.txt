"Microsoft has introduced updates to Azure AI Search that aim to assist organizations in constructing and expanding generative AI applications. These updates include new functionalities such as semantic search, knowledge mining, and improved cognitive search, resulting in more powerful and efficient AI-powered search experiences. These enhancements enhance scalability, performance, and cost-effectiveness for businesses using Azure AI Search. Numerous prominent companies, including OpenAI, Telus Health, and NIQ Brandbank, have successfully implemented Azure AI Search to improve their platforms and achieve better accuracy, responsiveness, and customer support.
