"NVIDIA and Hugging Face are partnering to provide access to NVIDIA DGX Cloud AI supercomputing on the Hugging Face platform. This collaboration aims to speed up training and tuning of large language models and create customized AI models for specific industries. Hugging Face will also offer a new service called Training Cluster as a Service, powered by NVIDIA DGX Cloud. The integration is expected to be available in the coming months. NVIDIA is collaborating with HPE to accelerate the advancement of generative AI models, and Hugging Face users will have access to NVIDIA's AI computing platform."
