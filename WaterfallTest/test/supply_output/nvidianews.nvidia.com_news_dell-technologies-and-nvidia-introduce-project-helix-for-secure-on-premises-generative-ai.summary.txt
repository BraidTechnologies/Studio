"Dell Technologies and NVIDIA have launched Project Helix, an initiative aimed at helping businesses build and use generative AI models on their premises. The project will provide tools, expertise, and infrastructure based on Dell and NVIDIA technology, enabling enterprises to deploy generative AI securely and effectively. This partnership will assist companies in enhancing various aspects of their operations such as customer service, market intelligence, and enterprise search. The solutions, combining Dell PowerEdge servers and NVIDIA H100 Tensor Core GPUs, will allow organizations to develop and deploy AI applications using their own data while ensuring privacy and security. Dell Validated Designs for Project Helix will be available in July 2023."
