"NVIDIA has launched its ACE generative AI microservices, enabling the creation of realistic digital humans. The suite includes speech recognition, text-to-speech conversion, translation, language understanding, response generation, and facial animation. NVIDIA has unveiled additional technologies such as body gesture generation and an on-device language model. The company aims to revolutionize industries with intent-driven computing. Dell Technologies has also introduced its Dell Generative AI Solution for Digital Assistants. NVIDIA showcased virtual avatars at COMPUTEX 2024, created using AI tools. The company is expanding its ACE platform and collaborating with various companies for different applications."
