"NVIDIA has released AI Workbench, a toolkit that simplifies the creation and customization of generative AI models. The software, part of the NVIDIA AI Enterprise 4.0 platform, is being adopted by companies like Dell and HP, and it includes tools like NeMo and Triton Management Service. NVIDIA aims to make generative AI more accessible for enterprises, and the AI Workbench will be available soon. NVIDIA also focuses on partnerships and finding the right infrastructure for generative AI."
