"Google has introduced LearnLM, a family of AI models designed to enhance learning experiences. It will be integrated into products like Search, YouTube, and Google Classroom. Google is also launching new tools, such as a learning coach, conversational AI on YouTube, and a program to simplify lesson planning. These initiatives aim to personalize and improve education. Google is inviting educators to test LearnLM and is partnering with institutions to develop online courses. The article also mentions Google's use of AI to reduce traffic congestion and emissions by optimizing routes."
