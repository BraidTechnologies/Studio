"Google introduced two new AI tools, Veo and Imagen 3, at Google I/O 2024. Veo generates high-quality videos, Imagen 3 creates photorealistic images from text. These tools were showcased through collaborations with artists and creators. Safety measures for AI-generated content were emphasized. Veo is currently available in private preview for selected creators. SynthID, a tool for watermarking AI-generated content, has also been released. Google is excited to see how people will utilize these tools. Language options for accessing keyword help are also mentioned."
