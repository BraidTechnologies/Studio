"SAP and NVIDIA are teaming up to integrate generative AI capabilities into SAP's cloud solutions. The partnership aims to offer custom generative AI services to SAP's enterprise customers, with a focus on automating various business processes. By leveraging NVIDIA's generative AI foundry service, SAP plans to enhance its offerings and provide developers with the tools they need to optimize code for specific industries. The collaboration also involves unifying AI data sources using SAP Datasphere, enabling customers to access high-quality data for AI and machine learning models. These new capabilities are expected to be available by the end of 2024."
