"VMware and NVIDIA have partnered to create the VMware Private AI Foundation, a platform that allows enterprises to integrate generative AI into their operations. The platform will provide a fully integrated solution with generative AI software and accelerated computing from NVIDIA. It will be built on VMware Cloud Foundation and optimized for AI. The partnership aims to provide privacy, choice, performance, data-center scale, lower cost, and rapid deployment. Dell Technologies, Hewlett Packard Enterprise, and Lenovo will also support the VMware Private AI Foundation. The companies plan to release the platform in early 2024."
